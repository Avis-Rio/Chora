# chora-style

A premium, intellectual aesthetic blending vintage woodblock typography with cinematic, atmospheric imagery. Inspired by "Huiwen Mingchao" (汇文明朝) and high-end cultural magazines.

## Visual Elements
- **Typography**: Dominant, vertical or elegant horizontal placement. MUST use **Traditional Chinese Mingchao/Songti (宋體)** with a slightly distressed, woodblock print texture.
- **Composition**: Clean, balanced, with a clear focal point. The text and image should feel integrated, like a book cover or movie poster.
- **Texture**: Rich, tactile textures. Paper grain, canvas, old stone, subtle noise, ink bleed.
- **Lighting**: Cinematic, dramatic, chiaroscuro (strong contrast between light and dark). Volumetric lighting (god rays) to add depth.
- **Detail**: High fidelity, photorealistic or hyper-realistic artistic render.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep, rich tones (Obsidian, Midnight Blue, Burnt Umber, Deep Forest Green).
- **Accent**: Muted Gold, Antique White, Vermilion (Chinese Red), Bronze.
- **Background**: Often dark or textured to allow the Mingchao text to pop in white or gold.

## Mood
- **Intellectual**: Serious, thoughtful, profound.
- **Historical**: Timeless, classic, enduring.
- **Atmospheric**: Mysterious, evocative, immersive.
- **Premium**: High-quality, curated, museum-grade.

## Best For
- Philosophy
- History
- Literature
- Deep Cultural Analysis
- Biography
